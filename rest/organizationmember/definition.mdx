An organization member is a [subject](../subject/definition) that belongs to an [organization](../organization/definition). It is created by other member of the organization.

A subject who's a member of an organization can access the resources of the organization, such as [signature requests](../signaturerequest/definition), [signature request invites](../signaturerequestinvite/definition), [links](../organizationlink/definition), [API keys](../apikey/definition) and [endorsables](../endorsable/definition).
